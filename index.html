About Us

Book My Yoga is dedicated to helping you achieve physical and mental well-being through the ancient practice of yoga. Our certified instructors guide you through each session with care and expertise.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced practitioner, we have classes tailored to your needs.

Our Services

Hatha Yoga

Traditional yoga practice focusing on physical postures and breathing techniques.

Vinyasa Flow

Dynamic sequence of poses synchronized with breath for a flowing practice.

Yin Yoga

Slow-paced style with poses held for longer periods to target deep connective tissues.

Prenatal Yoga

Specialized classes designed for expectant mothers to stay active and relaxed.
